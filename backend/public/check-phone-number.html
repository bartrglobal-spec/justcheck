Check a Phone Number for Scam Risk

Before sending money or trusting someone online, it's important to check whether a phone number has been linked to scams or suspicious activity.

Why You Should Check a Phone Number

Scammers often use phone numbers to build trust before asking for payments. These numbers may appear in online complaints, fraud reports, or suspicious listings.

Common Scam Scenarios

  • Online marketplace sellers asking for deposits
  • Investment opportunities promising fast returns
  • Unexpected messages requesting urgent payments
  • Unknown contacts claiming to be someone you know
