What this means
This phone number, email, or name appears in several public places online.

This does not mean the person is a scammer. However, when a contact appears in many public discussions or listings, it can sometimes be linked to online transactions.

If someone you do not know asks for money using this contact, it may be safer to verify their identity before sending payment.
What you can do next
• Ask the person requesting payment for additional verification before sending money
• Request a short phone or video call to confirm their identity
• Be cautious of urgent requests or pressure to send money quickly
• Consider using payment methods that offer buyer protection
Quick payment safety checklist
Before sending money, consider:

☐ Have you spoken to the person directly?
☐ Does the payment request feel rushed or urgent?
☐ Are you being asked to send money to a new or different account?
☐ Are you being asked to avoid normal payment protections?

If several of these apply, it may be safer to pause and verify before sending funds.
